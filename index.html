quick answers

Things people
usually ask.

Short-form videos and reels are usually done within 48–72 hours. Brand films and motion graphics take 5–7 days. Websites typically go live in 7–14 days depending on complexity. We agree on an exact timeline before we start — and we stick to it.
Yes — most of our clients are remote. We handle everything online: briefs, feedback, and file delivery. Time zones have never been an issue. We're available on WhatsApp and email, and we respond within a few hours during business days.
For video: your raw footage, any reference videos you like, and a brief idea of the feel you're going for. For web: your logo, brand colours, and a rough idea of what pages you need. For motion: a description of what you want to communicate and any existing brand assets. A rough idea is always enough to get us started.
Every project includes two rounds of revisions at no extra cost. In practice, most clients are happy after the first draft. We share work-in-progress previews early so there are rarely any surprises at the end.
Projects start from $299 for video editing and motion work. Websites start from $499. Pricing depends on scope — a quick reel is priced very differently from a full brand film. Send us a brief and we'll give you an exact number with no hidden costs.
You do — completely. Once the project is paid and delivered, all source files and final exports are yours. We don't hold files hostage or charge extra for originals. You'll also get a 14-day support window after delivery for any small tweaks.
