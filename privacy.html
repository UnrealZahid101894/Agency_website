Privacy Policy

// Last updated: January 2025  ·  Rendrx Studio, Dhaka, Bangladesh

What we collect

When you submit the contact form on our website, we collect your name, project type, budget range, and message. We also collect basic visitor data (page visited, referrer, browser type, and timestamp) for analytics purposes.

How we use it

Your contact details are used solely to respond to your enquiry. We do not use them for marketing, do not add you to any mailing list, and do not share them with any third party.

Visitor analytics data is used to understand how people use our site. It is not linked to any personal identity.

Where it's stored

All data is stored securely in Supabase (supabase.com), a hosted database platform. Data is stored on servers within the European Union. Supabase's privacy policy is available at supabase.com/privacy.

How long we keep it

Contact enquiries are kept for up to 12 months. Visitor analytics data is kept for up to 6 months. You can request deletion of your data at any time.

Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete any personal data we hold about you. To make a request, email us at hello@rendrx.com and we will respond within 7 days.

Cookies

We do not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies. We use localStorage to remember your theme preference (dark or light mode). This data never leaves your browser.
